Plant Care Tips

#1

Assess Environment

Evaluate the plant's placement in terms of light exposure, temperature, and humidity levels to ensure it matches the plant's requirements.
#2

Watering Schedule

Establish a consistent watering routine based on the plant's needs, allowing the soil to slightly dry out between waterings.
#3

Light Adjustment

Monitor the plant's response to its current light conditions and adjust its placement accordingly to optimize growth and prevent light stress.
#4

Maintenance Tasks

Regularly remove dead or yellowing leaves, dust the foliage and periodically check for pests or signs of disease.
